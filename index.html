What we talked about with the Client

1. Food coordinators, one for each ethnic food stalls. We have 5 groups: Chinese, International, Japanese, Korean and Indian food stalls. The food coordinators are also responsible to recruit parents to clean up at the end of the fair as well. This year, with the exception of the Chinese coordinator, all other coordinators were working parents.

2. Prize Corner: minimum 3 parents are needed for the shopping aspect. We need about 6 parents to set the toys up.

3. Exec: we are responsible to purchase disposables, take inventory count of the existing stocks of both equipment and disposables to be used for the fair. We have 4 exec member. Only one of our Exec is a housewife.

4. Decoration: We recruited about 6 parents to decorate the campus. I think most of them were elementary parents.

5. Set up: On the day of the fair, normally we will find few parents willing to help set up all the tables, equipment including chafing dishes, gas, stoves. We need to clean and wash all pots and pans before use. The major problem of doing an efficient job with the clean up & set up was the water supply. Although we purchased large plastic containers to move the water from the parking lot to the food area, it was very heavy and time consuming.

6. Hampers: one coordinator organized 8 hampers sold at the auction and it was a challenging job. The donations were very slow and at the end, we had used some of the items from prize corner and parents donation to be included into the hamper.

Conclusion from meeting Client

We have concluded that PTA needs 4 main things on our sign up sheet.

  1. Clean up
  2. Food Donation
  3. Preparation
  4. Set up

      Pros and Cons of using Dreamweaver
      Pros
    • - I can make positions like making a box stay where it is when my page is scrollable unlike firework
    • - The text on my page is easily copied(for my contact, parents might want to copy or just click on the email to send an email to contact)
    • - Really easy to use because it is the same thing as html, and it even shows you what it looks like on the side
    • - The option bar is easy to use it sorts out everything clearly and neetly allowing me to do things I cant do on Fireworks like making a position absolute ect.
    • - When I click on a box or anything on the visual side of dreamweaver it highlights the html on the other side which easily allows me to make changes the way I want

      Cons
    • - Gets really confusing when you make more layers because there is no layers to sort it out
    • - When making things the way I like it I have to research it to see where that option is located due to dreamweaver having so many options.
